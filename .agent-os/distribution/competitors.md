# ResumeBuilder — Competitors

## Direct

| Name | URL | Why people pick them | Our edge |
|---|---|---|---|
| Teal | tealhq.com | Strong brand + job tracker | Tighter focus on output quality; Hebrew authored |
| Rezi | rezi.ai | ATS framing | Confidence-building edits over rewrites |
| Kickresume | kickresume.com | Templates + builder | Job-tailoring tighter |

## Adjacent

| Name | What they are | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| ChatGPT-as-resume-tool | Free | Inconsistent output; no parser awareness |
| Canva resumes | Templates | Often fail ATS parsing |
| Career coaches | Human | Slower, costlier; sometimes a partner channel |
| LinkedIn resume builder | Default | Generic; we tailor by posting |

## Alternatives Users Try Before Us

- Free Word / Google Docs templates
- Their current resume "just edited"
- ChatGPT prompts copy-pasted in
- A career-coach friend
