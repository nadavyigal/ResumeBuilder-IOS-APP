# ResumeBuilder — Messaging

## Hero Phrases

- Web hero headline: "A resume that gets past the bots and sounds like you"
- Web hero subhead: "Tailor every application to the role, with ATS-aware edits and templates that actually parse."

## Capability Phrases

- Job tailoring: "Paste a posting, get a tailored draft in minutes"
- ATS-aware scoring: "See where the parser will trip — and how to fix it"
- Templates that parse: "Beautiful resumes that ATS systems can still read"
- Confidence edits: "Suggestions that keep your voice, not replace it"

## CTA Patterns

- Primary: "Build my resume" / "Tailor my resume"
- Secondary: "Score my current resume" (free tool)

## What Not To Say

- "Guaranteed interview"
- "Beat the ATS" (alarmist)
- "AI that writes your resume for you" (replacement vibe)
- "100% ATS pass rate"

## Hebrew Voice Note

Hebrew copy is authored, not translated. Tone matches Israeli job market norms. Job titles use local conventions. Date and education formatting follows local expectations.

## Email Subject Patterns

- Welcome: "Your resume workspace is ready"
- Didn't start: "Want a draft from a job posting?"
- Activation hit: "Resume exported — here's a tailoring tip"
- 14-day return: "Quick re-tailor for your next application"
